BMI Truth Table

The BMI (Body mass index) truth table (The one this calculator uses) provides a standard list of weight statuses based on BMI values. It helps you quickly identify whether your BMI falls within a healthy range or indicates potential health risks. This table is accurate for those 20 or older. Each BMI range corresponds to a category, from underweight to obesity, and reflects general health risk levels associated with weight. While this is a BMI calculator that serves information, it does not reflect the other factors of mass in your body, e.g. muscle mass, bone density, or fat distribution, so it should be considered along with other health indicators.
